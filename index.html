Guided nature walk - June 20th 2020 - 2pm

Border Park Kalmthoutse Heide

The Kalmthoutse Heide is a beautifull protected nature area, interesting in every season of the year. It is one of the oldest and biggest in Flanders. Come and check out the beautifull colors, animals and plants. It is an official silence area and ideal for a day hike.


The walk

Our guide Hilde will take you on a walk through the park where you will discover the animals that live there and learn a lot about the parks ecosystem.
